Here at iDigital

We are Pioneering the Next Era of Medical Discovery

Driven by Discovery, Guided by Precision

iDigital’s Medical Research Division combines neuroscience, robotics, and genetics to develop future-ready health technologies. At iDigital Medical Research, we lead surgical brain and neuro-robotics innovation, advancing precision technology and pioneering applied CRISPR breakthroughs.




Features we provide

1. Precision Robotic Brain Surgery:

Advanced robotic-assisted systems designed for minimally invasive brain procedures, enhancing accuracy, safety, and patient recovery time.

2. Brain–Computer Interface Innovation:

Cutting-edge BCI technologies enabling direct communication between the brain and external devices to support neurorehabilitation and assistive control.

3. Immersive Surgical VR Planning:

Next-gen virtual reality platforms that empower surgeons to visualize, plan, and rehearse complex operations in highly detailed 3D environments.

4. Strategic Research Collaborations:

We partner with academic institutions, hospitals, and biotech firms to co-develop groundbreaking medical solutions and expand scientific knowledge.

5. CRISPR & Gene Mutation Applications:

Research into gene-editing tools and methods, with a focus on CRISPR-based therapies targeting inherited diseases and cellular-level treatments.
